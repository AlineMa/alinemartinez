Loud

{{innovation_projects::details}}

#UI #WebApp #management

Context

During 2020, companies had to readapt the way they were interacting with theirs clients. While some business had fully solved their needs with platforms such as zoom, jestsy, etc, some, like LOUD had to tailor new solutions.

The problem

Loud is audio production business working in the field of publicity and movies. For the presencial meetings, they used to take their audio-material propositions from a local server in a pen drive and present it to their clients.

When remote presentation turned the "new normal" the Loud was having dificulties to present the sound refs to the clients because there are too many details to be discussed on the top of a song. It became difficult to ask the attention for some special instrument or rythm while the song-ref was being played online, for example. The team felt like they lost a bit of the presentantions' control.

"How can we have full control during the presentations?" became the question I should handle.

Needs Solution
transfer sounds from their cloud --> build a tailored presentation
not much time to organize presentations --> fetch intere folders from the cloud
take your own time to present the material --> control pannel + client's player off
comment tracks' details --> pause and play - back and forth
listen to the same track together --> player owner
respect client's privite meetings --> pass control to the clients = client's player on
many meetings in the same project --> organization with meetings inside projects
